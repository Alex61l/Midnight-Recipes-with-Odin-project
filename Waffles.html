Waffles with strawberry jam

For mass

  • 4 Spoons of sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups of flour
  • 2 Cups of milk
  • 1 Spoon of vanilla essence
  • 3 Spoons of yeast

Filling

  • 400g Frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cups of sugar
  • A lemon band
  • Maize starch

Method of preparation

  1. Place all the ingredients in a container and mix everything, put the dough in a waffle maker, and if you don't have one, put it in a frying pan with a little oil.

  2. For the filling,put the frozen strawberries in a pan and wait for it to melt until the strawberries are soft enough to mash. Once you've finished kneading the strawberries, add the sugar, a slice of lemon ,and to help thicken the filling, make a mixture of cornstarch with a little water and add it to the filling.

  3. Place the waffles on a plate and pour the strawberry jam on top, and that's it!
