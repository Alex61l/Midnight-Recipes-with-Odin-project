Passion fruit candy on the platter

Ingredients

  • Margarine
  • 1/2 Table cream
  • One condensed milk
  • 80ml Passion fruit juice
  • 1/2 passion fruit spare
  • Powdered milk
  • Chocolate

Method of preparation

  1. In a pan add margarine, a box of condensed milk, and half a box of table cream, 80ml of passion fruit juice, half a passion fruit and powdered milk. Mix everything with the heat on and stir until it starts to come away from the bottom of the pan. When finished, place the passion fruit brigadeiro in a glass dish.

  2. Melt a bar of chocolate, break the bar into pieces and place it in a glass jar, place it in the microwave for 30 seconds and stir, do this until the chocolate has completely melted.

  3. Pour the chocolate over the passion fruit brigadeiro and leave it in the fridge for 1 to 2 hours and that's it!
